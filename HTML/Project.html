A penguin is a flightless bird that lives in cold regions, especially Antarctica. Penguins are excellent swimmers and use their flippers to move through water. They live in groups and care deeply for their young. Penguins huddle together to stay warm in extreme cold. They are well adapted to survive harsh environments.

A dolphin is a smart and friendly sea animal known for its playful nature. Dolphins live in groups and communicate using sounds and movements. They are fast swimmers and often seen jumping out of the water. Dolphins are highly intelligent and can solve problems easily. They are also known to help injured members of their group.
